Frequently Asked Questions

What is Leago Academy?
Leago Academy is an AI-powered education platform that provides personalized learning for high school and college students. It offers step-by-step lessons, interactive quizzes, homework help, and exam preparation.
Is there a free version?
Yes! Leago Academy offers a free plan with limited AI lessons and basic quizzes. You can upgrade to Premium anytime for unlimited access to all features.
Which subjects are available?
We currently offer 12+ subjects including Mathematics, English, Life Sciences, Physical Sciences, History, Geography, and many more for both high school and college levels.
How does the AI Teacher work?
Our AI Teacher adapts to your learning style, providing step-by-step explanations, answering questions, and creating personalized practice problems to help you master each topic.
